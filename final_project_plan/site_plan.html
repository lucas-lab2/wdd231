Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

PetPledge Hub. This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the website's core function. "Pet" identifies the subject, "Pledge" signifies a commitment to donate, and "Hub" implies a central, comprehensive platform for connecting donors with animals and organizations in need.

Site Purpose

The purpose of PetPledge Hub is to serve as a trustworthy and centralized online platform that bridges the gap between generous donors and underfunded non-profit animal welfare organizations. The site will facilitate both monetary and in-kind donations, feature detailed profiles of abandoned animals seeking support, and provide a transparent tracking system to show donors the direct impact of their contributions. Ultimately, the goal is to increase awareness of animal welfare issues and make it easy for the community to provide help.

Scenarios

  • "How can I be sure my donation goes directly to helping the specific animal I want to support?"
  • "I don't have money to donate right now, but I have extra pet food. Is there a way for me to donate supplies instead of cash?"

Color Schema

  • Primary Blue (#0077b6): Used for main headings, links, and important informational text. It conveys trust, calmness, and professionalism.
  • Accent Orange (#fca311): Used for call-to-action buttons (e.g., "Donate Now"), highlights, and key interactive elements. It is warm, friendly, and draws user attention.
  • Dark Text (#14213d): Used for all paragraphs and body content to ensure high readability and contrast.
  • Light Background (#edf2f4): Used for the main body background to provide a soft, clean, and non-distracting canvas for the content.

Typography

The primary font for the entire website will be 'Nunito', served from Google Fonts.

  • Headings (h1, h2, h3): Will use Nunito with a bold weight (800) to be impactful and clearly define content hierarchy.
  • Body Text (paragraphs, lists): Will use Nunito with a regular weight (400) for optimal readability and a friendly appearance.
